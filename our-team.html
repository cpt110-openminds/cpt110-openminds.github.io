Hi! I’m Lachlan. I’m 28 years old and living in Sydney, Australia, though I grew up a little further north in Newcastle. Over the years I’ve completed a fair portion of a Bachelor of Communications, a little bit of a Bachelor of Business, and have a few TAFE-level certificates to top it off.

Personal Profile

Unfortunately for my team mates at Open Minds, my personal profile results point squarely towards "does not play well with others", despite what my best intentions might be around a particular interaction!

My results in the MBTI Test and the Four Tendencies, the results of which I've found to generally be quite accurate to how I am as a person, paint me as someone who has the capability to be quite disruptive if I disagree with logic or logistics behind a decision that's been made.

My tendency to critical analyse a situation can be a fantastic asset to any team because I can pick out potentially unforseen or overlooked issues or solutions. However, I need to be mindful to careless listen to and be open to proposed solutions from others rather than jump to a conclusion of an initial gut feel.
